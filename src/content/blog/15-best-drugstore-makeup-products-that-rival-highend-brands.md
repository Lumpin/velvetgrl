You don't need to spend your entire paycheck to achieve a flawless makeup look! I've discovered some incredible best drugstore makeup products that seriously compete with their high-end counterparts. These drugstore makeup dupes expensive brands will have you questioning why you ever spent $50 on foundation when the $12 version works just as beautifully.

## 1. Maybelline Fit Me Matte + Poreless Foundation

This lightweight foundation provides buildable coverage that lasts all day without looking cakey or settling into pores. The shade range is impressive, and the matte finish keeps oily skin looking fresh for hours without that dreaded midday shine.

**Style tip:** Mix a drop of facial oil with this foundation for a natural, dewy finish that rivals expensive luminous foundations.

## 2. L'Oréal Voluminous Lash Paradise Mascara

With its voluptuous brush and creamy formula, this mascara delivers dramatic length and volume that could easily pass for $30 luxury mascara. The formula doesn't flake or smudge, and it removes easily with makeup remover at the end of the day.

**Style tip:** Wiggle the wand at your lash roots and pull upward in a zigzag motion for maximum volume and separation.

## 3. Milani Baked Blush in Luminoso

This gorgeous peachy-coral blush has a subtle luminous finish that gives your cheeks the most beautiful healthy glow. The baked formula blends seamlessly and the pigmentation is so rich that one compact lasts for months.

**Style tip:** Use a fluffy angled brush to apply this blush along your cheekbones, then blend upward toward your temples for an instant lifting effect.

## 4. NYX Professional Makeup Butter Gloss

Creamy, non-sticky, and available in the most stunning shade range, these glosses feel luxurious on your lips without the designer price tag. They provide beautiful shine and just enough color to enhance your natural lip tone.

**Style tip:** Layer butter gloss over your favorite lipstick to transform any matte lip look into a glossy, dimensional finish.

## 5. Revlon ColorStay Liquid Eyeliner

This precision liner glides on smoothly and stays put through tears, sweat, and long days at the office. The felt tip makes it easy to create everything from thin, precise lines to bold graphic looks.

**Style tip:** Rest your elbow on a flat surface while applying to keep your hand steady and create the smoothest line possible.

## 6. CoverGirl Exhibitionist Stretch Mascara

This mascara lengthens and separates lashes beautifully without clumping, creating that coveted wispy, fluttery effect. The curved brush hugs every lash from root to tip, making even short lashes look dramatically elongated.

**Style tip:** Use this mascara on your bottom lashes for definition without the heaviness that can make your eyes look smaller.

## 7. Essence Pure Nude Highlighter

This buttery-smooth highlighter gives the most natural, lit-from-within glow that looks like expensive strobing products. The formula blends seamlessly and never looks glittery or overdone on the skin.

**Style tip:** Apply highlighter to the bridge of your nose and inner corners of your eyes in addition to your cheekbones for an all-over radiant look.

## 8. Wet n Wild Photo Focus Foundation

With full coverage that photographs beautifully and a formula that doesn't oxidize throughout the day, this foundation punches way above its weight class. It's buildable, long-wearing, and comes in an extensive shade range.

**Style tip:** Use a damp beauty sponge to blend this foundation for the most natural, skin-like finish that won't look heavy.

## 9. Flower Beauty Blush Bombs

These liquid blushes blend like a dream and give your cheeks the most natural flush of color. The buildable formula means you can go subtle for day or build up the intensity for evening looks.

**Style tip:** Dot liquid blush on the apples of your cheeks and blend outward with your fingers for the most natural application.

## 10. Colourpop Super Shock Shadows

These unique bouncy eyeshadows have incredible color payoff and a buttery texture that applies beautifully with your fingers. The metallic and shimmer shades especially rival high-end cream eyeshadows.

**Style tip:** Pat these shadows onto your lids with your ring finger for the most intense color payoff, then blend the edges with a fluffy brush.

## 11. Physicians Formula Murumuru Butter Bronzer

This silky bronzer smells like vacation and applies like butter, giving you a natural sun-kissed glow without any muddy or orange undertones. The formula is finely milled and blends seamlessly for a believable bronzed look.

**Style tip:** Use a large fluffy brush to apply bronzer in a "3" shape on each side of your face – forehead, cheeks, and jawline.

## 12. Almay Smart Shade Butter Kiss Lipstick

These pH-adjusting lipsticks adapt to your natural lip tone while providing moisture and a perfect wash of color. The formula feels comfortable all day and the shades are universally flattering.

**Style tip:** Apply this lipstick before bed as a tinted lip treatment to wake up with naturally enhanced, moisturized lips.

## 13. ELF Pure Skin Super Serum

This primer-serum hybrid creates the perfect smooth base for makeup while treating your skin with niacinamide and hyaluronic acid. It extends foundation wear and creates that expensive "no-makeup makeup" finish.

**Style tip:** Use this alone on no-makeup days for a natural, healthy-looking glow, or under foundation for extended wear.

## 14. Black Radiance True Complexion Concealer

This full-coverage concealer brightens under-eyes and covers blemishes without creasing or looking cakey. The formula is creamy yet sets well, and the coverage rivals much more expensive concealers.

**Style tip:** Apply concealer in an upside-down triangle shape under your eyes and blend outward for an instant brightening and lifting effect.

## 15. Jordana Easyliner for Eyes Retractable Pencil

This smooth, pigmented eyeliner glides on effortlessly and stays put all day without smudging or fading. The retractable design makes it travel-friendly, and the color selection includes both classic and trendy shades.

**Style tip:** Warm the pencil tip on the back of your hand before applying to make the liner even more creamy and blendable for smoky eye looks.

## Final Thoughts

These incredible drugstore finds prove that beautiful makeup doesn't have to break the bank! Each of these products delivers luxury-level performance at a fraction of the cost. Save this list and treat yourself to a few new favorites – your wallet (and your makeup bag) will thank you.
