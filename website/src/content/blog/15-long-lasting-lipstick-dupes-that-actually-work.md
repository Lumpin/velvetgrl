## 1. NYX Soft Matte Lip Cream vs. Kylie Cosmetics Liquid Lipstick

![NYX Soft Matte Lip Cream in deep berry shade with smooth application](/images/posts/15-long-lasting-lipstick-dupes-that-actually-work/01.png)

The [NYX Soft Matte Lip Cream](https://www.nyxcosmetics.com) delivers the exact same velvety finish and impressive 6+ hour wear as Kylie's cult-favorite formula, but for a fraction of the price. The pigmentation is incredibly rich, and shades like "Budapest" and "Prague" are dead ringers for some of Kylie's most popular colors. Plus, the doe-foot applicator makes precision application effortless, even without a lip liner.

**Style tip:** Apply in thin layers and let each dry completely for the most comfortable, transfer-proof finish.
